What is Medifaces ???

Medidata is growing fast. Take a walk in any of our offices and you'll see a new face. Medifaces is a little game that helps us to get to know each others names and job functions.

How do I win?

Every time you identify a fellow Medidatian you get 1 point.

As you gain points you will gain levels with the ultimate goal of getting to AWESOME. Getting to AWESOME is by no means easy.

How do WE win?

It's more fun to work at a company where everybody knows your name.

What are these ?

The game works when everyone plays fair and adds an image of themselves that people can recognize. It breaks down when people upload avatars and other images that make them unrecognizable.

If you are asked to recognize a player and they're not playing nice you can click the image to mark them as Toxic. This will lock them out for 24 hours, during which time they will hopefully upload a recognizable picture of themselves into Medidata Express.

The scores don't seem to add up?

Although we don't show the scores achieved by ex-employees on the main scoreboard we do still count them toward departmental and location totals. That's why a departments score can be higher than the sum of its (apparent) individuals.
